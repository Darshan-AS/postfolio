# Privacy Policy for Postfolio

*Last Updated: 2026-06-20*

Postfolio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Authentication Data**: We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts (Google Sign-In).
- **Financial Data**: Data you enter about customers and deposits is stored in Firebase Firestore and is scoped strictly to your user account.
- **Usage Data**: We collect anonymous usage statistics via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to improve the app.

## 2. Data Storage & Security
All your data is stored securely using Google Cloud infrastructure. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## 3. Offline Data
Data is stored locally on your device for offline access and synced to the cloud when a connection is available.
