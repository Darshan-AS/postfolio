## Decisions & Conventions
- **Agnostic First**: All new customizations are placed in `.agents/` rather than IDE-specific folders like `.vscode/` or platform-specific ones like `.github/`. This ensures the tools work across various AI agents (Copilot, Cursor, etc.).
- **Deterministic Scripts**: Prefer shell scripts for versioning to ensure consistency across different developer environments.
